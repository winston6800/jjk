Jujutsu Kaisen is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Gege Akutami, serialized in Weekly Shonen Jump since March 5, 2018. The story follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student who becomes involved in the world of curses and jujutsu sorcerers after swallowing a cursed object.

When Yuji's friends are attacked by curses, he swallows a finger belonging to the King of Curses, Sukuna, to save them. Now sharing his body with Sukuna, Yuji must work with jujutsu sorcerers to collect all of Sukuna's fingers and prevent him from regaining his full power.
